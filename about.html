Who is Banele Mareletse?

Banele Maud Mareletse is a lot of things. To some, a caring women. To others, she might be seen as ruthless. All I know is that I am her and I am happy being me. I'm a creative, aiming at bringing a breath of fresh air to the world. Sometimes (most times) I'm too caring. I'm too understanding. I want to understand, but sometimes it comes at the cost of self. Honestly I'm not sure what to write here. I could list all my personality traits but that's not me. I am the awareness, and I guess as the awareness I'm not choosing to lie to you now and say I want to write things for professionalism sake. I want to write the things I want to write and that's all. Why lie to the world by being professional? I want to be real. Anyway, that's a short looking into how I think. Hope that helped you figure me out.
